# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please report it privately by emailing **support@sugra.systems**.

Do not open a public GitHub issue, pull request, or discussion for security vulnerabilities. Public disclosure before a fix is published can put users at risk.

## What to include

- A clear description of the issue and its potential impact.
- Reproduction steps or proof-of-concept (if available).
- The affected component, version, or commit hash.
- Your contact details for follow-up.

## Response timeline

- Acknowledgement of your report: within 2 business days.
- Triage and initial severity assessment: within 5 business days.
- Remediation plan or timeline: within 7 business days for confirmed vulnerabilities.

We coordinate disclosure with the reporter and aim to credit researchers in release notes unless anonymity is requested.

## Scope

This policy covers code maintained in this repository. Vulnerabilities in upstream dependencies, third-party services, or operating system components should be reported to their respective maintainers; we will assist in coordination where appropriate.

## Out of scope

- Findings from automated scanners without demonstrated impact.
- Social engineering of Sugra Systems employees, contractors, or users.
- Denial-of-service attacks against production infrastructure.
- Issues that require physical access to a user's device.

## Other channels

- Legal inquiries: legal@sugra.systems
- Privacy and GDPR rights: privacy@sugra.systems
- Acceptable Use Policy violations: abuse@sugra.systems
